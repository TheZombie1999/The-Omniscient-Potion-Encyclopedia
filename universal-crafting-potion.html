Universal Crafting Potion

Crafting a universal potion that enhances both Smithing and Enchanting can only be crafted with Springan Sap. In Addition you need one of these Pairs of items.

  • Blisterwort and (Hagraven_Claw, Spawn_Ash or Snowberries)
  • Sabre_Cat_Tooth and (Snowberries Hagraven_Claw or Spawn_Ash)
